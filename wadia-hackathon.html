🌟 “Behind every line of code, there’s a story of doubt, persistence, and discovery.” I recently participated in the 2nd National Level Project Competition, presenting our project: 🛒 "Optimizing Retail Inventory with Multi Agents". What started as a technical challenge became a journey of learning, growth, and teamwork.

Problem: Retail stores face a constant balancing act: ➡️ Stockouts lead to lost sales and unhappy customers 😞 ➡️ Overstocking results in high storage costs 📦💸

Goal: Build a multi-agent AI system capable of: 🔮 Predicting product demand 📦 Maintaining ideal inventory 💰 Optimizing pricing in real-time 🔗 Coordinating between stores, warehouses, and suppliers

My Role: Demand Forecasting Agent Data preprocessing became key: cleaning missing values, encoding variables, scaling features, creating lag features, and hyperparameter tuning. Result? A model with ~99.75% accuracy and deep respect for well-prepared data.

Key Learnings: - Collaboration is essential; our team—Sneha Gupta, Juideepa Das, Omkar Bholankar, and I—handled different agents and challenges. - Data preprocessing is the foundation of any AI system. - Balancing academics, project work, and life taught us perseverance and time management.

💬 This competition wasn’t just about showcasing a solution—it was about becoming a team that can solve real-world problems. Trust the process; even mistakes shape you into a better engineer and person.
